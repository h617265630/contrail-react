## Design Context

### Users
- **Self-learning developers** (自学开发者): Technical, self-directed, exploring on their own
- **Context**: Late-night study sessions, commute,碎片化时间学习
- **Goal**: Turn scattered resources into structured, actionable knowledge and trackable progress

### Brand Personality
- **Professional & trustworthy** (专业可信) — Linear/Vercel-grade precision, not playful
- **Focused** — calm, no noise, every pixel earns its place
- **Technical credibility** — signals "built by developers who care"

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Linear/Vercel reference**: stone/stone palette, minimal decoration, soft shadows, generous whitespace
- **Light-mode dominant**: clean, bright, professional — no dark mode by default
- **Rounded corners**: rounded-2xl for cards, rounded-xl for buttons — approachable but not cute
- **Soft shadows**: shadow-sm/shadow-xl — depth without heaviness

### Design Principles
1. **Trust through restraint** — No decorative flourishes. Whitespace communicates confidence.
2. **Developer-grade polish** — Consistent 4px grid, tight typography, no visual competition
3. **Amber as signal** — Single accent color for primary actions only; everything else stone/neutral
4. **Progressive disclosure** — Complexity hidden until needed; don't dump features on first view
5. **Motion with meaning** — Hover lift, loading pulse — functional only, no animation for drama

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Inter/system-ui, font-bold, tracking-tight — confident, no serif flourish
- **Body**: Inter, text-base, text-stone-500 — readable, not chatty
- **Scale**: 14/16px body, 32/40/48px headlines

### Spacing Scale
4px grid: 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96
